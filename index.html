Privacy Policy for Discord Bot Integration

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the data we collect when you use the Discord bot integration with our platform, how we use it, and how we protect your personal information. By using our services, you acknowledge and accept the practices described here.

2. Data Collection

By using our Discord bot integration, we may collect certain data from your Discord account, depending on the permissions you provide. We gather this information to enhance your experience, as well as to detect and prevent potential scams or malicious activity. This data may include:

  • User Profile Information: Information such as your username, avatar, unique Discord ID, and any public profile details.
  • Server Information: Data about the servers you are part of, including roles, permissions, and server-specific settings.
  • Message Content: Content from your interactions with the bot, such as commands and queries, to process your requests and provide relevant services.
  • Friends and Connections: Information about your friends or other server members to enable social features.
  • OAuth Scopes: The specific data that may be accessed based on the permissions granted, which include:
    • openid: Enables Single Sign-On functionality.
    • profile: Grants access to read your profile information.
    • asset: Allows viewing information about your assets.
    • creator-store-product: Allows you to view information about your Creator Store products.
    • group: Allows viewing information about your groups.
    • legacy-universe.following: Grants access to read information about the experiences you follow.
    • universe.subscription-product.subscription: Grants access to read about your subscription products.
    • universe.user-restriction: Allows you to view user restrictions in the experience.
    • user.advanced: Grants the ability to read the user's premium and verified status.
    • user.commerce-item: Allows viewing information about your commerce items.
    • user.commerce-merchant-connection: Allows reading information about linked merchant accounts.
    • user.inventory-item: Allows viewing information about your inventory.
    • user.social: Allows you to view a user's social accounts.

3. Use of Data

We use the data collected through the Discord bot integration for the following purposes:

  • Service Enhancement: To personalize and enhance your experience on the platform by tailoring features, commands, and interactions to your preferences.
  • Support and Issue Resolution: To assist you when you contact us via Discord for support, and to resolve technical issues related to your use of our bot.
  • Analytics: To analyze how users interact with our bot, which helps us improve functionality, add new features, and maintain service quality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent, except under the following conditions:

  • Legal Compliance: When we are required by law to disclose information.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party services to assist with technical operations, hosting, or analytics, and these providers may have access to certain data only to fulfill their roles.
  • Anonymized Data: We may share anonymized and aggregated data with partners for analytical or research purposes, ensuring no personally identifiable information is disclosed.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard practices to secure your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include encrypted storage, limited access controls, and regular security audits. However, it is important to understand that no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. User Control and Rights

Revoke Permissions: You have the right to revoke access to our bot at any time by removing it from your Discord server.

Data Deletion: You may request the deletion of personal data collected through this integration by contacting our support team through our Discord server.

Consent Withdrawal: You may withdraw your consent to our collection and use of your data at any time, although this may limit your access to certain features.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed towards individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware of such data being collected without parental consent, we will take steps to delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or legal obligations. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page and providing a revised "last updated" date.
